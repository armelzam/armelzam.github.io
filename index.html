What is pacefaint?

pacefaint is something you'd get if a beauty brand had a baby with a hypebeast brand. pacefaint is a line of cosmetics, primarily focused on eyeshadow and eye primer, both of which are applied by hand, hence “pacefaint.” The products are meant to compliment your fit, whether its for color-coordination, or just adding a pop of color to your OOTD. Every streetwear junkie strives for the freshest fit from head to toe, but sometimes they forget that their head includes their face!
